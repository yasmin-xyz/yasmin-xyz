## Projects

**[UFC Fight Predictor](https://github.com/yasmin-xyz/ufc-fight-predictor) (Pick'em Labs)** — A sports betting research tool for UFC fights. Pulls live odds from multiple sportsbooks so users can compare market value across fighters, combines that with real fight stats and metrics pulled from custom ESPN and UFC Stats scrapers, and runs the matchup through multiple LLMs (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) in parallel to compare their predictions side by side. Solves the problem of bettors having to manually check several sportsbooks and stats sites separately with no single view to spot value.

**[Kora](https://github.com/yasmin-xyz/movewithkora)** — An AI class planner designed for yoga instructors. Instructors set a class length, skill level, and peak pose, and Kora generates a full structured flow — warm-up, build, peak, counterposes, cool-down — with breath cues and modifications. Solves the problem of instructors spending hours each week manually planning sequences instead of teaching. React/TypeScript with a Supabase backend and streamed AI generation. 

**[PickMeLabs](https://github.com/yasmin-xyz/PML)** — An AI visibility research lab for ecommerce. As more shoppers ask AI tools directly for product recommendations instead of searching Google, brands have little visibility into or control over whether they get recommended. PickMeLabs researches how AI models surface and rank products, and helps ecommerce brands understand and improve their visibility in AI-generated answers.
